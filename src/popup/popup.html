    Remove Chrome print header/footer

    1. Open the Chrome print dialog.
    2. Click “More settings”.
    3. Uncheck “Headers and footers”.
    4. Optionally, enable “Background graphics”.

    These instructions affect the current browser; they are not specific to the extension.
